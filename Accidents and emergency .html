Clinic Operation hours

The Pearl Hospital has an Accident & Emergency Department, which is manned round the clock by professional staff trained in advance life support. The staff are well equipped to handle complex trauma and emergency medical cases, with the support of medical specialists and the appropriate medical experts as required.
The Accident & Emergency Department comes fully equipped for medical accidents and emergency care, with Ambulances, Radiology Services, Laboratory Services, Pharmacy Services and the Day Surgery Unit.

Pedriatic Unit
