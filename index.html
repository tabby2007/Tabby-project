About the Hospital

Opened in 1973. The Pearl Hospital is known for its good and hardworking doctors. It was once voted as the second best hospital in East Africa. The doctors and all staff believe and know that the patient's health is a priority. Expansive development in latest technology and medical equipment that has enabled us establish leadership in medical procedures in both Kenya and outside.
